How the agents operate — the loop

A loop that runs whether or not anyone is watching.

    Sense

    Each counterpart watches the feeds behind its mandates — continuously.

  2. 2

    Find

    When reality drifts, it raises a finding and traces the impact up to the intent it threatens.

  3. 3

    Decide
